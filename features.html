#Features

Appointment and Customer Management

Manage all your appointments with flexible calendar displays and layouts. Handle all the customer information and their booking history more easily than ever.

Services and Service Providers

Set up your services and the employees/teams/sections which will serve the customers. Each service is completely configurable and every provider has his own schedule.

Business Workflow and Booking Rules

Configure the system at your own will, apply the business hours and breaks and let customers book, modify or cancel appointments within specific time periods.

Google Calendar Sync

Synchronize your events with Google Calendar and share them with other applications. Providers and secretaries will be able to schedules their data as well.

Email Notifications

Get email notifications for any change on the schedule including bookings, modifications and cancellations. Customers are also informed in time for changes in their appointments.

Stand Alone Installation

Install your own copy on your server and use the source code and database information in any way you want. Easy!Appointments is open and free!

Translated User Interface

Use the application in your own language! Easy!Appointments is currently translated into 21 languages which are updated and maintained from the community. New translations are being added all the time.

User Community

Get in touch with other enthusiasts from all over the world and share your experiences concerning modifications, troubleshooting or just ideas exchange.

Multiple Attendants

Enable mutli-attendant slots and handle groups of customers at certain times. No worries, Easy!Appointments can do that too!

Dedicated Provider and Service Links

Use dedicated URLs in your website that can navigate your customers to book appointments with specific services and/or providers.

Responsive Design

Launch the app from your mobile phone and handle all the UIs flawlessly in any viewport size.

REST API

Develop rich content applications by using the flexible REST API endpoint and handle all the application-wide information from your clients.

#Translations

Easy!Appointments supports localization and many users have contributed to the project with their translations! The user interface is currently translated into English, German, Greek, Portuguese, Spanish, Dutch, Japanese, French, Chinese, Polish, Italian, Luxembourgish, Danish, Hungarian, Slovak, Russian, Finnish, Romanian, Turkish, Hindi and Bulgarian! You can add your own language or use one of the existing available on this page.

#Get Involved

The most important part of Easy!Appointments is its community and users. By taking an active role in the support group, testing pre-release packages, providing and maintaining translations, making PayPal donations and sending pull requests on GitHub you provide great assistance to the improvement of the project. Read a more detailed guide on GitHub.

#Future

Easy!Appointments aim is to become a full-featured and extensible booking system solution which will make easier the way people interact with each other as far as appointments are concerned. Every release is carefully designed and implemented in order to achieve the best possible result. The future of Easy!Appointments is bright and will remain as always open.
